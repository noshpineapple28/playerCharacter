    An old ear, dried and almost unrecognizable except for its shape. The ear represents the crest of house Corendeck. Although apparently centruies old, the ear still bleeds, and a faint pulse can be felt when held onto it. ENTER_LINE The ear represents the knowledge and connection of the realm encapsulated by house Corendeck. Those who use the houses name in vain are subject to desounding, the practice of removing any organs involved in listening to or conveying information. ENTER_LINE Those that make a vow to the crest are granted a part of the vast web of connections that encapsulates Corendeck.
